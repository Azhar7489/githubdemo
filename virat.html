In 2013, Kohli was ranked number one in the ICC rankings for ODI batsmen. In 2018, he was ranked top Test batsman, making him the only Indian cricketer to hold the number one spot in all three formats of the game. He is the first player to score 20,000 runs in a decade. His cover drives are just awesome.

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket team.
